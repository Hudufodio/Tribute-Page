USMAN DAN FODIO

Philosopher and Islamic Scholar

1754 - 1817

ORIGEN

Usman Dan Fodio was a fulani scholar,islamic religious teacher,revolutionary and philosopher who founded sokoto caliphate and ruled as its first caliph. Born in gobir,Usman was a descendant of the Torodbe clans of urbanized ethnic fulani people living in the hausa kingdoms since the early 1400s.
